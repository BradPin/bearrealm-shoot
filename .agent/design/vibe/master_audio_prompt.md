# Master Audio Prompt Vault

> This file contains the foundational style guidelines and prompt templates for all AI-generated audio assets.
> **AGENT DIRECTIVE**: Always reference these guidelines when generating SFX or BGM.

## Global Audio Vibe
**"High-Energy Fantasy Arcade"**
- **Core Acoustic Identity**: A blend of bright, magical chimes with heavy, satisfying physical impacts. It should feel snappy and responsive (Arcade/Casual RPG feel), matching the bright vector art style.
- **Tonal Balance**: Avoid overly dark, realistic, or gritty cinematic audio. Prioritize "juicy" and satisfying sounds—think pinball mechanics mixed with fantasy spellcasting.

## Mixing Guidelines
- **BGM (Background Music)**: 
  - Upbeat, adventurous, and looping.
  - Instrumentation: Orchestral foundations (brass, strings) mixed with modern synthetic beats to keep the energy high.
  - Mix: Kept relatively mid-focused so high-frequency SFX (coins, magic hits) and low-frequency SFX (heavy collisions) cut through clearly.
- **SFX (Sound Effects)**:
  - **Impacts**: Needs a strong "thump" (low-mid) followed by a crisp "clack" or "smash" (high).
  - **Magic/UI**: Bright, clear, high-frequency "sparkles", "whooshes", and "pops".
  - **Decay**: Short and snappy. Keep reverb tails tight so the audio doesn't become muddy when multiple marbles are bouncing simultaneously.

## Prompt Templates

### 1. Combat & Physics SFX (Collisions / Slingshot)
**Base Prompt**: `A short, punchy sound effect of [Subject/Action: e.g., heavy stone colliding / tight elastic snapping], arcade fantasy style, snappy, strong impact, dry with no long reverb, high quality game audio`

### 2. Magic & Elemental SFX (Faction Skills)
**Base Prompt**: `A bright, energetic sound effect of [Element/Action: e.g., fiery explosion / magical healing chime], fantasy RPG style, crisp high frequencies, satisfying "pop", clean game audio`

### 3. UI SFX (Clicks / Transitions / Rewards)
**Base Prompt**: `A satisfying, juicy UI [Action: e.g., button click / coin collect] sound, casual mobile game style, bright, short, snappy, positive tone`

### 4. Background Music (BGM)
**Base Prompt**: `An upbeat, adventurous fantasy background music track for [Setting/Mood: e.g., a lively battle arena], orchestral with modern punchy beats, heroic, energetic, seamless loop, casual RPG style`
